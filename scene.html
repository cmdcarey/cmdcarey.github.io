Seattle Craft Coffee

The City Scene

Downtown Seattle is home to several local small roasters, easily accessible from I-5. Most locations offer tours and tasting sessions; some even offer basic classes in roasting. Of course, roasters sell their coffee and associated swag. Large, wholesale purchases are also welcome on a case-by-case basis.
